About AquaGuard

AquaGuard is a platform designed to simplify complex marine data
and make ocean conservation easy to understand for everyone.

What is SDG 14?

SDG 14, Life Below Water, is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations in 2015. It aims to protect marine ecosystems by reducing ocean pollution, preventing overfishing, and conserving coastal and marine areas.
Oceans are essential for human survival as they produce over half of the world's oxygen, regulate global temperatures, and support a rich diversity of marine life. However, increasing pollution and climate change are putting these ecosystems at risk, making SDG 14 more important than ever.

Our Mission

At AquaGuard, our mission is to raise awareness about ocean conservation, highlight threats to marine life, and empower individuals
to take action. We aim to make complex marine data simple, engaging, and actionable for everyone.
